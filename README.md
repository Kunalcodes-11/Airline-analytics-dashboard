# Airline-analytics-dashboard
Airline Analytics Dashboard An interactive dashboard analyzing airline performance, passenger trends, delays, and revenue to provide actionable insights for operational efficiency and business growth.

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze airline operations and passenger data to gain insights into flight performance, passenger trends, and revenue generation. This dashboard helps stakeholders optimize operations, improve customer experience, and make data-driven business decisions.

❓ Questions (KPIs)
What is the total number of flights, passengers, and revenue?
Which routes have the highest traffic?
Which airline has the best on-time performance?
What are the most delayed routes or airports?
Monthly trend of passengers and revenue
Which class (Economy/Business) contributes most to revenue?
Peak travel periods and seasonal trends
Cancellation and delay percentage
⚙️ Process
Verified dataset for missing values and handled inconsistencies
Cleaned and transformed data (data types, formats, null values)
Created calculated measures (Total Revenue, Delay %, Load Factor)
Built pivot tables / visualizations based on KPIs
Designed an interactive dashboard using slicers/filters (Airline, Route, Time, Class)
Combined all visuals into a dynamic and user-friendly dashboard

📈 Project Insight
Certain routes show consistently high passenger traffic, indicating strong demand
Delays are concentrated in specific airports and peak travel periods
Revenue is largely driven by specific airlines and premium class passengers
Seasonal trends show spikes in travel during holidays and vacation periods
Some routes have high cancellations, impacting customer satisfaction
Load factor varies significantly across routes, highlighting optimization opportunities
✅ Final Conclusion

To improve airline performance, companies should focus on optimizing high-demand routes and reducing delays at key airports. Enhancing operational efficiency during peak travel periods can improve customer satisfaction and reduce cancellations.

Additionally, promoting premium services and improving route planning can maximize revenue and increase overall efficiency.
